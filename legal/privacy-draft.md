Stradigi AI ("Stradigi AI," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. ## 2. Scope and Our Role

We act in two different capacities depending on the information involved:

- **As a Controller** — for information we collect about visitors, prospects, and account administrators through our website, marketing activities, and account management (e.g., billing contacts, support requests).
- **As a Processor / Service Provider** — for Customer Data that our business customers ("Customers") submit into the Service in the course of using it — including task inputs, documents, prompts, and any personal data Customers choose to route through Agents. In that capacity, we process such data only on the Customer's instructions and under the terms of the applicable Terms of Service and, where executed, a Data Processing Agreement ("DPA"). If you are an individual whose personal data has been submitted to the Service by one of our Customers, please direct privacy requests to that Customer; we will assist them in accordance with our contractual obligations.
